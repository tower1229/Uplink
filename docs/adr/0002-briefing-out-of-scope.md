# Briefing and topic aggregation are out of scope

The requirements doc describes cross-Platform topic aggregation and background packs (Briefing / `contexts/`). Those are deferred as downstream data-use problems. This Repository's domain stops at durable Evidence, Imports, Captures, Conversations, and Messages — so first-version language, schema, and CLI do not treat Briefing, Topic, or Context-pack as first-class terms.
